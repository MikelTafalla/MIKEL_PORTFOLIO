My Story

The story of my academic and professional life has always moved in a circle soon to be closed. My first attempt to pursue a university degree was to study computer science. However, at the moment, I dreamt with playing professional soccer and I was in a good track to believe in my possibilities. MISTAKE. After crashing with reality, I embarked myself on the study of language. I studied Modern Languages in Spain and got lucky enough to receive a scholarship at UCSB to pursue an MA in Iberian Linguistics, which I obtained on 2016. My time as a student and after was marked by the experience I got teaching Spanish at the University level. Yet, deep down, I knew I wanted to do something different.
So...
Here we are now, studying coding again, hoping to close the circle and make a living as software engineer
